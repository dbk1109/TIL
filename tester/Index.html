RESTORING THE FOUNDING SPIRIT AND IDENTITY
나는 자랑스러운 대한민국 국민입니다.

FREEDOM MATTERS "Build Up Korea 2023" is the 1st annual U.S.-Korea Conservative Conference for the Next Generation of Koreans taking place in Seoul, Korea. The event aims to bring together U.S. politicians, influencers, and pastors to discuss the theme of "Restoring the Founding Spirit and Identity." The conference will focus on exploring ways to reconnect with the values and principles that formed the basis of the Korean and U.S. founding values and ideals, with a particular emphasis on how the younger generation can play a role in shaping the future of the world.
